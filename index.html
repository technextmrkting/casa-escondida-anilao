Our Story

A Hidden Paradise
in Batangas

Nestled along the shores of Brgy. Ligaya in Mabini, Batangas, Casa Escondida — "The Hidden House" — is an intimate resort and professional dive center where world-class diving meets genuine Filipino warmth.

Anilao is the birthplace of Philippine scuba diving and a legendary macro photography destination. Our resort places you at the heart of this underwater kingdom — vibrant coral gardens, rare critters, and breathtaking marine biodiversity await.

Dive Into Anilao's

Getting Here

Find Your Way to Anilao

Located in the heart of Anilao — the Philippines' diving capital — Casa Escondida is easily reachable from Manila for a perfect weekend or week-long escape.

Address
Brgy. Ligaya, Mabini, Batangas Province, Philippines 4202
From Manila Airport
88.5 km · Approx. 2–3 hours by car via STAR Tollway → Batangas City → Mabini
By Bus
JAM Liner or Ceres from Buendia → Batangas, then tricycle or jeepney to Anilao
