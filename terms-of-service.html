Diving Requirements & Safety

Participation in dive activities at Casa Escondida is subject to the following requirements. These conditions are in place for your safety and the safety of our dive team.

  • Certification requirement: All divers participating in guided open-water dives must hold a valid PADI Open Water certification (or equivalent recognized certification from NAUI, SSI, CMAS, or similar agencies). Proof of certification card must be presented before any dive activity commences.
  • Medical fitness declaration: All participants must complete a standard PADI Medical Statement prior to diving. Certain medical conditions may require a physician's clearance before diving is permitted. This is for your safety — our staff cannot override this requirement.
  • Age requirements: The minimum age for PADI Open Water diving is 10 years old. Divers under 18 must have written parental or guardian consent on file.
  • Non-certified guests: Guests without certification may participate in our Discover Scuba Diving (DSD) experience under direct instructor supervision, to a maximum depth of 6 metres. The same medical form requirements apply.
  • Dive briefings: All divers must attend the pre-dive briefing conducted by the assigned divemaster or instructor. Guests who miss the briefing may be excluded from that dive without refund.
  • Dive master authority: Our divemasters and instructors have full authority to refuse, curtail, or terminate a dive if, in their professional judgment, conditions are unsafe or a guest is unfit to dive. No refund will be issued for dives terminated due to guest behavior or unsafe conduct.
  • Intoxication: Guests who appear to be under the influence of alcohol or controlled substances will not be permitted to dive. This is a non-negotiable safety policy.

Resort Rules & Conduct

To ensure a peaceful and enjoyable environment for all guests, the following rules apply throughout the resort property:

  • Quiet hours: Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. Loud music, gatherings, and activities that disturb other guests are not permitted during these hours.
  • No outside food and beverages: Outside food and beverages are not permitted to be consumed in common dining areas or beach spaces. Guests may bring personal snacks to their rooms. Please respect our restaurant and bar operations.
  • Smoking policy: Smoking is strictly prohibited in all enclosed spaces, including rooms, the restaurant, and the dive center. Designated smoking areas are provided at the resort perimeter. Guests who violate this policy will be charged a cleaning fee of PHP 2,000.
  • Environmental responsibility: The use of non-reef-safe sunscreen is prohibited at the resort and on all dive boats. Single-use plastics are discouraged. We ask all guests to treat the marine environment with respect.
  • Marine protected areas: All guests must adhere to local MPA rules, including no touching, collecting, or damaging coral, marine life, or sea floor structures. Violations may result in fines under Philippine law.
  • Property damage: Guests are responsible for any damage caused to resort property, dive equipment, or vehicles. The cost of repair or replacement will be charged to the responsible guest.
  • Firearms and illegal substances: The possession of unlicensed firearms or illegal substances on resort property is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal and reporting to local authorities.
  • Pets: Pets are not permitted on the resort property, with the exception of certified assistance animals with appropriate documentation.

Health & Medical Conditions

Certain medical conditions may disqualify a guest from participating in dive activities. These include, but are not limited to: recent surgery, asthma, epilepsy, heart conditions, uncontrolled diabetes, and certain ear or sinus conditions. The PADI Medical Statement completed prior to diving will identify any conditions requiring physician clearance.

Guests with known dive injury risk factors are strongly advised to discuss this with their physician and with our dive staff prior to booking.
