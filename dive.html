Where Every Dive Begins

Casa Escondida's PADI-certified dive center sits steps from your room. Our experienced divemasters and instructors know every reef, crevice, and critter in Anilao — so whether you're logging your first dive or your thousandth, you're in expert hands.

Hot showers, full equipment rental, camera rinse stations, nitrox fills, and a private jetty for direct access to Anilao's legendary dive sites make every morning effortless.
