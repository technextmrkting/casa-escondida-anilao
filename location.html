Travel FAQs

Before You Set Off

Questions we hear often from guests planning their trip to Anilao.

What's the nearest airport?
Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA / MNL) in Manila is the main gateway. The resort is about 130 km south — roughly a 2.5 hour drive. We can arrange a private van transfer from NAIA with 48 hours notice.
Do you offer airport pickup?
Yes — private air-conditioned van transfers from NAIA, Makati, BGC, or anywhere in Metro Manila. Please contact us with your flight details at least 48 hours in advance.
Is the trip doable in a single day?
Technically yes — but we strongly recommend at least 2 nights. Anilao has 30+ dive sites minutes from our pier, and the quality of diving here is worth the journey. Most dive guests stay 3–5 nights.
Is there parking on-site?
Yes. Free on-site parking for all guests, directly within the resort gate. No reservation needed — just drive in and our staff will direct you.
When is the best time to visit?
Anilao is divable year-round. The dry season (November to May) has the calmest seas and best visibility. For macro enthusiasts and underwater photographers, the "muck season" from November through March is legendary.
Can I visit without diving?
Absolutely. Many of our guests come for the ocean-front rooms, pool, Filipino cuisine, and quiet coastal setting. Non-divers are just as welcome — we have guided snorkeling, island hopping, and nearby attractions to explore.
Is there mobile signal and WiFi?
Globe and Smart both have reliable 4G signal in the area. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the resort — rooms, restaurant, dive center, and common areas.
